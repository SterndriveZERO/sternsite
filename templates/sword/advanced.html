🎯 Punish Crit Reaction

Reaction Risk ⭐⭐ Reward ⭐⭐

Objective

Recognize whether the opponent is going for Sprinthits, Punish Crits, or Double Crits and counter instantly.

Key Idea

Punish Crit timing is slightly more delayed than Sprinthits because of falling hit timing.

🎯 NoKB Combo

Advanced Risk ⭐⭐⭐ Reward ⭐⭐⭐

How to Perform

Use NoKB Crit into Jump Reset to maintain position and continue pressure.

  • Counter Crit Deflection
  • Win air trades
  • Maintain combo pressure

🎯 Falling Sprinthit Deflection

Counter Risk ⭐⭐ Reward ⭐⭐

How to Perform

Let opponent crit you, then respond with a falling sprinthit instead of a crit.

  • Works best in crit trades
  • Creates immediate combo starter

🎯 Crouch Dodge

Escape Risk ⭐ Reward ⭐⭐

How to Perform

Crouch right before enemy swing to reduce hitbox and dodge attacks.

  • Best as last resort
  • Weak vs Punish Crits

🎯 Stun-Hit Prediction

High Skill Risk ⭐ Reward ⭐⭐

How to Perform

Hold S at the exact moment before an incoming stun hit lands to break combo flow.

  • Can completely stop combos
  • Requires timing prediction
