⚔️ Hit Types

There are 5 different hits, all with individual sound.

  • Sweep Hit – 90%-100% charged, cancels sprint, AoE damage.
  • Sprint Hit – 90%-100% charged while sprinting, extra knockback.
  • Critical Hit – 90%-100% charged while falling, bonus damage + particles.
  • Pick Hit – 0%-90% charged, lower damage/knockback.
  • Standard Hit – Jump hit without sprint, not useful in combat.

📝 Game Rules

  • Reach – Max range is 3 blocks
  • Momentum – Forward = more range, back = less range
  • Knockback Rule 1 – Sprint facing target reduces KB
  • Knockback Rule 2 – Taking KB + sprinting reduces KB by 40%
  • Crouch – Reduces hitbox size
  • Priority – Sprint hits beat most other hits

🧑‍🏫 Terms

  • First Hit – First hit in neutral position
  • Hit Advantage – Ability to hit first in a trade
  • Hit Priority – Control gained by landing first hit
  • Stun – Opponent cannot hit/move temporarily
  • Knock Away – Sprint hit that pushes opponent back
  • Double Hit – Hit after knockback without being hit
  • Crit Chain – Multiple crits in a row
  • Stun Combo – Multiple stun hits in a row
