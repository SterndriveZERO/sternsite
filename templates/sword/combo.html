🎯 Combo Fundamentals

Core Concept

Combos rely on hit timing and spacing. Maintaining around 3 block distance with correct timing allows long stun combos.

  • If you hit too fast → opponent KB cancels
  • If you hit too slow → opponent walks out of combo
  • Ping affects optimal timing

⚔️ Winning Trades vs Combos

Decision Making

Winning trades gives 1–2 hits advantage. Combos allow multiple hits without taking damage.

→ Always prioritize combos over trades when possible.
