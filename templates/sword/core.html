⚔️ Sprinthits

Stun Technique Risk ⭐⭐ Reward ⭐⭐⭐

How to Perform

Sprinthits deal more knockback and are meant to stun the target. To keep chaining them, you must reset sprint between hits.

  • W-Taps – Release W after each hit to reset sprint.
  • Pausing W-Taps – Delay W slightly for stronger knockback control.
  • S-Taps – Best spacing control for maintaining 3-block range.

The goal is to maintain spacing so the opponent never regains hit priority.

⚔️ Standard Crits

Damage Risk ⭐⭐ Reward ⭐⭐

How to Perform

Jump and hit while falling. Release W before hitting to cancel sprint and turn the hit into a crit.

  • Late W release = more range, less control
  • Early W release = more control, less range

Use this in combos or punish situations.

⚔️ Crit Deflection

Knockaway Risk ⭐⭐ Reward ⭐⭐

How to Perform

Counter to Standard Crits. Sprint hit the opponent immediately after they land a crit on you.

If timed correctly, it will knock them away and break their combo.

⚔️ Punish Crit

Damage Risk ⭐⭐ Reward ⭐⭐

How to Perform

After getting knocked back, use the momentum to land a crit while falling.

Best used as a follow-up into Double Crit or NoKB setups.

⚔️ NoKB Crit

No Knockback Risk ⭐⭐ Reward ⭐⭐⭐

How to Perform

Hold W after getting hit, then delay your next hit to reduce knockback.

  • Follow up with Punish Crit to maintain pressure
  • Or use Falling Sprinthit to re-stun opponent

Proper spacing is required or the combo will break.

⚔️ Double Crit

Damage Risk ⭐⭐ Reward ⭐⭐

How to Perform

Counter to NoKB Crits. After being NoKB critted, punish and immediately jump into a standard crit.

Only works if opponent commits to NoKB pressure.
