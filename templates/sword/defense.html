🪃 Hit Dodging

Defense

1. Dodging First Hits

Walk back right before opponent reaches range to avoid the hit. Timing + distance is key.

  • Best used in defensive playstyles
  • After dodge → go for stun combo or crit

Countered by: Falling Sprinthit

2. Baits

Move forward slightly to bait attacks, then instantly back out to dodge. This forces mistakes and creates hit advantage.

3. Turn Back Movement

Turn away briefly when opponent attacks, then instantly re-engage.

  • Can be used mid-trade
  • Best followed with crits

🏃‍♂️ Escaping Stun Combos

Survival

Methods:

  • Hold S – Most common escape method
  • S-Hold Jump – Increases knockback (use vs high ping)
  • Turn and Run – Full disengage escape

🚀 Movement

Spacing / Control

Movement is used to force misses and create distance for stun hits.

  • Make opponent miss hits
  • Create spacing for combos
  • Works best with crits, sweeps, sprinthits

Warning: Excess movement can increase knockback and lead to being comboed.

⭐ Strafe

Movement Style
  • Short Strafe – fast A/D movement
  • Side-to-Side – switch direction every hit
  • Circle Strafe – rotate around opponent

Strafing makes you harder to hit but increases knockback taken.

Use only when you cannot be punished or when spacing for a stun.
