⚔️ Hit Select

Stun / NoKB Risk ⭐⭐ Reward ⭐⭐⭐

How to Perform

Let the opponent hit you, then immediately sprint hit them back. If timed correctly, this creates a stun and reduces knockback by 40%.

  • Best when already in close range trades
  • Can counter Sprinthits and S-Hold deflection

⚔️ Uppercut

Stun Technique Risk ⭐⭐ Reward ⭐⭐⭐

How to Perform

Hit the opponent while jumping forward.

  • Used to close long distances quickly
  • Can be used as combo extension
  • Works well with W-tap / S-tap combos

⚔️ Falling Sprinthit

Knockaway Risk ⭐⭐ Reward ⭐⭐⭐

How to Perform

Perform a sprinthit while falling.

  • Confuses opponents expecting a crit
  • Works well for combo extension
  • Strong vs defensive players holding S

⚔️ S-Crits

Spacing Risk ⭐⭐ Reward ⭐⭐⭐

How to Perform

Tap or hold S after a crit to create spacing advantage.

  • Creates hit advantage
  • Can be used for combos or escape
  • Strong when opponent is airborne

⚔️ S-Hold Deflection

Stun Technique Risk ⭐⭐ Reward ⭐⭐⭐

How to Perform

After a Crit Deflection, hold S to reposition and extend range.

  • Best vs NoKB crit pressure
  • Only works defensively

⚔️ Repositioning

Spacing Risk ⭐⭐ Reward ⭐⭐⭐

How to Perform

Adjust your position using movement keys (mostly S) to maintain optimal range.

⚔️ Jump Reset

NoKB Risk ⭐⭐ Reward ⭐⭐

How to Perform

Jump at the moment of being hit to reduce knockback.

  • Best vs sprinthits
  • Bad vs delayed timing or stun hits

⚔️ Pick Hit

Damage Risk ⭐⭐ Reward ⭐⭐

How to Perform

Hit before your crosshair is fully charged (0–90%).

  • Good for finishing combos
  • Can interrupt opponent movement

⚔️ Sprint Crits

Damage Risk ⭐⭐⭐ Reward ⭐⭐

How to Perform

Cancel sprint hit, then immediately jump and crit.

  • Harder version of Standard Crits
  • Less control, higher risk
