📊 Risk to Reward Ratio

🔹 Risk Level

The chance of getting countered while attempting a technique.

🔹 Reward

  • Low Reward – Small advantage (example: free hit with no control after)
  • Medium Reward – Helps escape bad situations
  • High Reward – Gives hit priority or full advantage
