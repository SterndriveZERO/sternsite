‼️ Overview

Your playstyle should always be flexible. When fighting someone for the first time, use a safer approach. Once you learn their patterns, you can adapt and counter them.

📋 General Playstyle Tips

  • Focus on first hits to gain control.
  • Try to crit more often than ground hit.
  • Avoid getting critted for free.
  • Release W late for max reach crits or NoKb crits.

🥊 Playstyle Structure

⚖️ Equal Fight

If both players engage at the same time, aim for Standard Crits or early counters like S-Crit or Deflection. Be ready to hold S mid-trade.

🔥 When You Are Aggressing

  • Standard Crit: Wins most trades unless deflected.
  • NoKb Crit: Strong control, but risky vs S-Hold or Double Crit.
  • Ground hits only when close or catching opponent off guard.

🛡️ When You Are Defending

  • Use hit dodging to gain first hit advantage.
  • If hit, respond with Crit Deflection or S-Hold Deflection.
  • Close range → Crit Deflection
  • Mid/long range → S-Hold Deflection

❗ Adaptation

Once you understand your opponent’s habits, you can break the standard structure and counter them directly. PvP is about reading, not memorizing.
