⚔️ Overview

Sweeps are control-based attacks used to manipulate spacing, dodge timing, and punish aggressive play. They rely heavily on sprint control and hit priority.

🪃 Core Sweeping Methods

🏃 Sprint Sweep

Hold W + sprint. First hit is a sprint hit, following hits become sweeps until sprint resets.

🔁 W-Release Sweep

Release W before every hit → hit → press W again. Used for control and spacing during combos.

🪃 Sweep Techniques

1. Single Sweep

Used to force misses and break opponent aim. Best at close range or after dodges.

2. Sweep Combo

Strafing with sweeps makes your movement unpredictable and harder to track. Strong when opponent already missed a hit.

3. Punish Sweep

Counter to hit select. Sweep → bait hit → sprint hit back for stun.

4. Backaway Sweep

Sweep into S-Crit to disengage while maintaining control.

5. Followup Sweep

Used to extend combos by catching S-hold escape attempts.

6. Back Sweep (Trade Counter)

Used when both players crit at the same time. Sweep after their hit to gain stun advantage.

7. Side Sweep

Anti-rush tool. Used against aggressive players to break their aim and reposition.

📌 Key Idea

Sweeps are not about raw damage — they are about controlling timing, spacing, and opponent movement.
