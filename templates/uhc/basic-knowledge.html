🔰 Basic Knowledge

There are many different items in the UHC kit. Some of the basics you will need to know how to use are the following:

  1. 🛡️ Shield — Used to properly evade damage from the opponent and to provide better opportunities for good trades.
  2. ⚔️ Sword (Sharp 4) — Useful to deal damage, keeping distance from your opponent, and mine webs faster.
  3. 🪓 Axe (Sharp 1) — Used mainly for disabling your opponent’s shield or doing extra damage; can be used to stun web.
  4. 🍎 Golden apples — Used to gain 2 hearts of absorption and 2 hearts of normal regen; used to keep your health high.
  5. 🕸️ Cobwebs — Useful to trap your opponents, perfect for aggroing them when they have low health, or trapping them so they can’t chase you.
  6. 🌊 Water — Extremely useful for extinguishing yourself, breaking webs, and keeping your opponent stuck in water.
  7. 🪣🔥 Lava — Used to help slow your opponent, damage your opponent, redirecting your opponent’s attention to extinguishing themselves, and draining them.
  8. 🏹 Bow — The bow is great for dealing damage to bring it in your favor, breaking their momentum, and doing it from a distance.
  9. 🎆 Crossbow — Crossbowing is perfect for dealing ranged damage through shields, essentially doing the same as the bow.
  10. 🪵 Blocks — Great for removing their water, protecting yourself, and helping to aggro.
  11. ⛏️ Pickaxe — Used to mine cobblestone quickly.

📝 Game rules

  • Reach — The max range is 3 blocks with your melee weapons, but 4 blocks with your water and lava. (In 1.20.6+, it is 4 blocks. In 1.20.5 and below, it is 4.5.)
  • Knockback — If you sprint while facing the target, you take less knockback. (Works whilst jumping too.)
  • Range — If you jump or run away from your opponent, or get knocked back, your range is lowered.
  • Crouching — Decreases your hitbox size.

📃 Common terms

  • First hit — The first hit you land on your opponent whenever you’re engaging (typically before they hit you).
  • Crit chain — Getting multiple crits in a row.
  • Spacing — How well you distance yourself from your opponent.
  • Stun — When you double click your mouse to give your opponent significant vertical KB.
  • Stun webbing — When you stun your opponent, and place a web beneath them.
  • Drains — Removing your opponent’s waters.
  • Floppy arrow — Bowing with low charge.

⚙️ Useful mods

Here, mods that are important or useful will be posted below.

  • Player health indicators — Very useful for taking good opportunities to pressure, and nice for knowing what they are at.
  • Perspective / freelook — Not to be confused with freecam; this lets you orbit around your character, essentially having F5 that points in any direction you want.
  • Crosshair indicator — Useful to see when your opponent is in your reach; helps your spacing.
