🌟 Gamesense

One of the most important aspects of UHC is Gamesense, as it doesn't always necessarily matter which player has more skill, as a player with less skill but more Gamesense often has a better chance of improving.

Overview

Gamesense in UHC consists of various aspects, all of which play a significant role in quickly improving. These include gap timing, shielding, and, above all, spacing.

↔️ Spacing

The best and easiest way to improve your spacing in UHC is to play Sword, as this will get you used to different situations and strats that most UHC players don't pay attention to, making it extremely easy to outspace them.

🛡️ Shielding

Another very important aspect in UHC is shielding. Many players still use their shield in very unfavorable situations or simply not at all, which puts them at a huge disadvantage. The best way to shield perfectly is to incorporate the shield into your spacing. First, it is important to know that it is never a good idea, especially for less advanced UHC players, to first block at the start of a round, as this usually gives your opponent a free shield break, which can put you at a huge disadvantage. A good way to use your shield effectively is to sprint hit your opponent and then shield, as they will usually respond with a hit select, which you can then easily block.

It is also always a good idea to insta shield, which means hitting your opponent and immediately shielding afterwards. This applies to both crits and normal sweep/sprint hits.

To use your shield as effectively as possible, you should always make sure to be the first person to break your opponent's shield, as this allows you to deal maximum damage to your opponent without taking the same amount of damage.

🍎 Gap timing

Another big mistake many players make is healing at the wrong times, which leads to them taking a lot of unnecessary damage. Roughly speaking, you should never heal when you're taking damage. However, there are a few things you should pay attention to. Firstly, you should only heal when you have enough distance between yourself and your opponent, as this means they can't hit you while you're healing. However, they can still shoot you with a bow or crossbow, so if you have the time, always place blocks in front of you, i.e. build a wall in front of you, so they can't shoot you. You should only heal when you're taking damage if you're about to die but still have a high healing advantage over your opponent.

⚔️ Aggressive / defensive playstyle

In order to gain a lot of health over your opponent, it is helpful in many situations to play aggressively, as this can take your opponent by surprise and allow you to take a lot of health away from them under pressure. However, you should keep in mind that it is rarely a good idea to play aggressively when you have a big advantage over your opponent, as in such situations it is simply better to play safely without losing your advantage, as there is a chance that you will die more quickly when playing aggressively. The same applies the other way around, because you should never play defensively if you have a big disadvantage compared to your opponent, as this allows them to easily play the whole game safely without having to take any risks.

Summary

The best way to quickly get good gamesense is to simply think about how you are playing and what you could or should do in certain situations while you are playing.
