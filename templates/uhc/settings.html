Comfort first

If you are already consistent on a sensitivity and keybind layout, do not reset everything for UHC. Tune small things (FOV, toggle sprint, shield slot) instead of chasing “perfect” numbers you cannot muscle-memory yet.

Aim & look

  • Mouse DPI + sensitivity — Pick a combo where you can track bow strafes and snap to melee without overshooting. Most players land somewhere in ~800–1600 DPI with moderate in-game sens.
  • FOV — Higher FOV helps awareness; very high values can distort depth for rod and bow. Find a stable value and keep it.

UHC-specific habits

  • Hotbar order — Sword, rod, bow, shield, gaps, lava/water on slots you can hit blind under pressure.
  • Toggle sprint — Extremely common so sprint management in trades does not eat focus.
  • Reduced particles / clear hitsounds — If allowed on your client, cleaner feedback helps read trades (rules vary by server).
